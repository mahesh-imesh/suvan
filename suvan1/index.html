Overview

A New Era Has Risen. Join Along!

Suvan Cresta, positioned at the pinnacle of Bibvewadi, offers you a unique habitat manifested through magnificent design at every niche and bay. Taking the legacy of the neighbourhood many notches higher, literally! From up here, you can see Pune breathe!

We present to you, homes which are Upscale but Unpretentious, Contemporary yet Trendy, Functional as well as Aesthetically Enticing.

At Suvan Cresta every detail has been carefully thought upon before transforming it to veracity.

At SUVAN, we take all the possible initiatives in every aspect we deal with to leave a footprint of which we and our future generation will be proud of. A unique Solar-Wind Hybrid System at Suvan Cresta takes care of the project’s power consumption needs, in a clean, responsible and right way.

Community is the foundation to success. And the 80+ happy members of the Suvan Family at Suvan Cresta will definitely tell you no different.
